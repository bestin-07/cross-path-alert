Welcome to AMISAFE

Using this website, you can check weather you have crossed paths with a COVID-19 patient





How It Works


Sign up and Login to your AMISAFE account.


Once you are inside the user's dashboard, enter your travel history from your memory. The data that we collect are the 'time-of-visit', 'date-of-visit', 'place-of-visit'. The more accurate the data you give the better results can be output. We respect your privacy, so we only use the data given by you. We store this data for only 30 days, after which it is removed.[feature pending]


We have a separate database containing the travel history of COVID-19 positive-tested patients, available through public sources like newspaper, television, social media posts(of authorities). We collect these data manually and enter into our database. The data is as accurte as provided by these sources.


Using the your travel history data provided by you, we cross check it with our patient's travel history database. And based on the date, time and location we determine a risk-value for each of your 'place-of-visit', which can be seen in the user's dashboard.


The risk-value is color coded for easy comprehension.

Green is SAFE

Amber is MODERATE RISK

Orange is HIGH RISK

Red is VERY HIGH RISK

Based on your over-all risk value a summary instruction message to follow will be provided on your dashboard.


We request you stay indoors for both yours and everybody's continued safety. Follow social-distancing and self-isolation to flatten the curve. But if you went outside for some reason and wants to know whether you could have crossed paths with a possible COVID-19 patient then all you have to do is login to your account and input your data [back to STEP 1]

The predictions made are as accurate as the date provided in the sources. So we advise you not to trust the site blindly.
